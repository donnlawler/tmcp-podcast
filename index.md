# The Minimum Commitment

## Film Theory in Small Doses

A podcast about movies, meaning, and the ideas hiding beneath the surface.

Each episode takes one film and one theory, then breaks it down in a way that feels clear, useful, and connected to the experience of watching.

## About the Show

The Minimum Commitment is built for listeners who love film but do not always have time for a full lecture.

The episodes are short.

The ideas are serious.

The goal is simple: help you watch films with sharper eyes and deeper attention.

## About Donn

Donn Lawler is an academic writer, educator, audio engineer, and podcast host based in Austin, Texas.

He holds an MA in Film and Media Studies from Arizona State University and has worked in sound, film, video, and podcast production for decades.

His work focuses on film theory, genre, sound, meaning, and the ways movies help us think through the world.
